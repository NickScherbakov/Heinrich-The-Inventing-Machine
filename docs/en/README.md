## About Heinrich

Heinrich combines classical TRIZ (Theory of Inventive Problem Solving) methodology with modern Large Language Models to provide systematic, interpretable inventive problem-solving capabilities.

Named after Genrich Altshuller, the creator of TRIZ, Heinrich embodies the systematic thinking approach of the original methodology while leveraging contemporary AI advances.

## Key Features

- **Systematic TRIZ Pipeline**: 7-module workflow following classical TRIZ methodology
- **Interpretable Reasoning**: Every step is logged and traceable to TRIZ principles
- **Knowledge Base**: Complete 39 parameters, 40 principles, contradiction matrix
- **Multilingual**: Full support for English, Chinese, Russian, and Arabic
